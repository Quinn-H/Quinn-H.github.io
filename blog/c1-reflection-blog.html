Reflection

   

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

The learning experience in DBC/EDA is very different to the traditional school. DBC/EDA not only focus on student’s technique skills, but also teaching them soft skills. This is my first week in EDA, I can feel I am in a supportive learning community with people who have same goals. I feel more confident to explore new world when I studying and working in a team.


What are your impressions?

I really enjoy the EDA’s learning guideline; the learning materials are organized very well.


How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I am keeping my mind open and looking forward to go far with team.


Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

No, this is exactly what I’ve been looking for.


Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment?

Does it make you nervous?

I am so excited to participate in this learning environment. I don’t feel nervous.
