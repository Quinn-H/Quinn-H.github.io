Daily Mindfulness

   

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

My partner felt it is difficult to focus at beginning when I practiced mindful listening with her. I felt it is really helpful to our relationship.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Looping and dipping are most useful technique. Once the speaker has completed his speech, then you should tell the speaker what you understood from his speech. If speaker finds you misunderstand something from his speech, then the speaker will let you know the errors and you will express again to the speaker. This process will repeat until speaker satisfied.
