Emotional intelligence

   

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

After I read the Search Inside Yourself chapter 1. I realize the emotional intelligence is very important factor to becoming a successful IT professional. How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I feel energized, deep relax and mind peaceful after two minutes of mindfulness. It is very good way to heal your mind when you under pressure of work. I will do it every week.
