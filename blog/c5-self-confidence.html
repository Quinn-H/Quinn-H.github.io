Self-Confidence

   

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

From definition that given by Daniel and Jon, I think mindfulness and self awareness are very similar. Mindfulness is aware of the present moment and what we’re doing. I personally look at self awareness is more boarder than mindfulness that can be focussed on both past and present.

Try at least one of the self-awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience with it? How did you feel?

I think the body scans help me a lot and easy to do. I feel deeply relax after body scan. The technique of body scan is started to focus on your foot first, then go through your entire body.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I am not emotions; emotions are experience in my body. I agree with it because emotions like a cloud in my body. Unpleasant emotions like the dark cloud and it will bring a big storm to you but it is not you.
