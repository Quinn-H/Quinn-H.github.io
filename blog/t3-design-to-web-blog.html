What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important.

A responsive site will always fit your device's screen. The layout of the site will adjust automatically. It will provide an optimal viewing experience with end-user across a wide range of devices.

What mobile first design is, and why it's important.

Mobile first design means what it says. This is a user experience consideration that must be made at design time. It is important because more and more users using mobile as their first choice to visit website. A research found 25% of U.S user are mobile only, and up to 45% of Rural China internet user are mobile only in 2013. This number will continue growing as time goes.
